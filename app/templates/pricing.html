Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and features

Can I change my plan anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

What happens if I exceed my upload limit?

If you exceed your monthly upload limit, you'll be prompted to upgrade your plan. Your analysis history is preserved when you upgrade.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! We use bank-grade encryption for all data transfers and storage. Your resume files are automatically deleted after processing, and we never share your data with third parties.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team for a full refund.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access to your plan features until the end of your current billing period.
